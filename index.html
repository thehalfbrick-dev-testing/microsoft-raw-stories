About Us

The Half Brick is a Global Community

Founded in 2021,

Section 8 Non-Profit Organization

TheHalfBrick® have created an inclusive people’s platform named as raw stories to inspire our younger generation in the form of storytelling, to recognize and celebrate the life journey of the self-made and selfless people who are growing from ground zero from all walks of life around the world. Our motto is to create “self-dependent younger generations” to achieve that we are going to upskill and cross skill our Unemployed Graduates, Graduates, students, College dropouts, school dropouts and working professionals.

Microsoft x TheHalfBrick Raw Stories

At Microsoft, we honor the remarkable journeys of self-made and selfless women who are shaping the future of digital business. These trailblazers come from diverse backgrounds, rising from ground zero to transform industries, inspire communities, and redefine what it means to lead with purpose. Join us as these inspiring women share their life stories, reflecting on the challenges they faced, the setbacks they overcame, and the resilience that propelled them forward. Their experiences remind us of the true meaning of success: perseverance, innovation, and giving back. Together, let’s celebrate their achievements and amplify the voices of women who are creating a more inclusive and connected digital world.
